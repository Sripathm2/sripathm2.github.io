Intrusion detection for UAV networks where the topology, traffic, and threats change in real time. **FlowRAG** uses retrieval-augmented generation so the detector generalizes to unseen attacks without constant retraining.

**My role:** end-to-end — problem framing, model design, training, and adversarial evaluation.

**Stack:** Python, PyTorch, retrieval-augmented generation, federated learning.
